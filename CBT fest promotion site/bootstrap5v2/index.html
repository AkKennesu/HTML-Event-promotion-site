About PSU CBT Fest

Business Technology Fest has been a resounding success, filled with the infectious energy of students cheering passionately during the parade. The theme 'Tech Forward: Pioneering Innovation for Tomorrow's Business World' is brought to life by the responsible facilitation of our instructors, students, and officers

Their dedication sets the stage for a series of events that not only showcase technological innovations but also foster a spirit of community and forward-thinking. Let's continue to ride this wave of enthusiasm and inspiration as we pave the way for a brighter, tech-empowered future!

DOIT

Provide sufficient support for the BS Information Technology students of Pangasinan State University Alaminos City Campus.

SYBA

SYBA is the authorized organization of students from the Business Administration programs of PSU Alaminos City Campus.
